For our Parents

  • Be Aware of Eco-Anxiety: Some environmental topics can be overwhelming for young children.
    Be prepared to offer reassurance and discuss ways children can make a difference.

  • Limited Interaction Features: If the platform has interactive features,
    ensure they are limited and age-appropriate.

  • Parental Controls: Set up parental controls on your devices to limit access to inappropriate content on the internet.( settings)

  • Communication is Key: Talk to your child about online safety basics like not sharing personal information with strangers.
    Encourage them to come to you with any concerns.

  • Set Time Limits: Establish clear time limits for platform use to promote healthy screen time habits.
